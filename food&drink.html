Places to buy food and drink on campus

Wave kitchen

Overview

At the wave kitchen, they serve all kinds of freshly made food from sustainable sources. Located on the ground floor of the Wave, they will most certainly have what you want.

Food

Their breakfast deal comes with anything from pastries to pancakes while the more satisfying lunch has the likes of loaded nachos and fresh salad.

Specials

their signature dish, the “Muffuletta sandwich” is a beast, filled with layer after layer of pesto, mozzarella and cured meats and veggies, made from fresh sourdough, you will be sure to enjoy it.

Jessop cafe

Overview

At the Jessop cafe, they serve a variety of hot drinks, and food prepared in house just for you. Located in the Faculty of Arts and Humanities at Jessop West, they will be sure to have your choice of beverage.

Food

They have many breakfast amenities such as poached eggs and tasty avocado for the perfect healthy breakfast, or even pastries and cakes for a quick snack in the afternoon. And if your lucky, they even have a porch outside for when the weather isn't miserable.

Specials

Their Smashed avocado over pristine toasted sourdough is the most popular breakfast in all of Jessop west, and you have got to go try it out, and if your craving some protein, then they have optional bacon and egg for a small extra fee.

Diamond kitchen

Overview

At the Diamond building, they have a multitude of different international dishes which can be eaten in house or as a take out. Located at the entrance of the Diamond building, there's not a chance that you won't leave without a bite to eat.

Food

Due to the large menu of mainly international dishes, they can almost guarantee that you will find something that you like no matter what culture you come from.

Specials

Their famous Grill & Go burritos, will be sure to stimulate your palate, not to mention topped fries and jacket potatoes, this signature dish will be the perfect choice to get you though a long day of lectures and labs.

IC cafe

Overview

At the IC cafe, They serve hot meals and snacks throughout the day and night. Located on the ground floor of the Information Commons library building, it is open whenever you need so you will be sure to have something to eat during your study sessions

Food

There is a range of food available from curries with rice or burgers and chips, there is no doubt that you will have something that you like when it comes to a hot meal. There are even cold meals such as sandwiches and wraps and a range of both hot and soft drinks for you to indulge in.

Specials

Their signature dish which is a homemade curry, is the talk of the University where you can add a portion of rice or chips for a small fee. It is definitely worth your money however, and you have got to give it a go.
