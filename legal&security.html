Legal and security

Measures to ensure security

  • To ensure compliance with the relevant legal bodies, every image that I have used, has been under either the creative commons license, where I have referenced them appropriately, or I have made it clear that it is a copyright image.
  • To ensure that I am complying with GDPR, I have included a privacy policy linked to the page, which the user has to sign off when filling in the contact form.
  • Due to the nature of the website being local and not on any public domain, when including images, it is not necessary that they be copyright free, however I have put in the utmost care to ensure most of my images where possible are under a creative commons license.

Privacy Policy

At SheffBites, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any personal information you provide. This policy outlines what information I collect and how I use it.

1. Information I Collect

I may collect basic personal information, like your email address, when you contact me or fill out forms on my website.

2. How I Use Your Information
