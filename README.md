# SheffBites

## Overview
SheffBites is a student-focused website designed to help University of Sheffield students and visitors discover places to eat and drink around campus.  
The site features curated food and drink recommendations, nearby picnic areas, and an interactive quiz, combining personal opinions with researched information.  
The design is fully responsive and accessibility-aware, ensuring a smooth experience across desktop and mobile devices.

## Features
- **Food & Drink Guide:** Curated recommendations for places to buy food and drink on and around campus.  
- **Picnic Areas:** Highlights nearby outdoor locations suitable for eating and relaxing.  
- **Interactive Quiz:** A fun quiz based on famous statues and figures found in Sheffield parks.  
- **Responsive Navigation:** JavaScript-powered hamburger menu for mobile and smaller screens.  
- **Accessibility Focused:** Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, alt text, figure captions, and form labels.  
- **Multi-Page Site:** Includes accessibility, design, testing, and legal & security pages.
